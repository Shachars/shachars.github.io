About me

I am an algorithm researcher at Huawei's Tel Aviv Research Center, working on wireless communications algorithms for WiFi. I research diverese PHY and MAC topics such as: iterative MIMO detection algorithms, Deep Reinformecment Learning for WiFi congestion control and more.
I also teach the graduate level course “Digital Transmission of Signals” in the EE school in Tel Aviv university.
I've spent over a decade working on algorithms for communications and sensing at Intel, Vayyar Imaging, Corning Optical Communications, General Motors Research and Apple.
I hold a PhD (“Information Theoretic Active Learning”) in Electrical Engineering, which I completed under the supervision of Prof. Meir Feder.
