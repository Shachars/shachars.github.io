About me

I am studying towards my PhD in Electrical Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Meir Feder. I am currently working on the relationship between Information Theory and Machine Learning (In particular active and online learning).
I am also a researcher at Apple, working on wireless sensing algorithms.
I've spent several years working on algorithms for communications and radar systems at Intel, Vayyar Imaging, Corning Optical Communications, General Motors Research and Apple.

Research

My research interests include

  • Machine Learning
