About me

I am studying towards my PhD in Electrical Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Meir Feder. I am currently working on the relationship between Information Theory and Machine Learning (In particular active and online learning).
I am also a part time radar researcher at General Motors Advanced Technical Center Israel (GM-ATCI), working on future sensing systems for autonomous vehicles.
I've spent several years working on algorithms for communications and radar systems at Intel, Vayyar Imaging, Corning Optical Communications and General Motors.

Research

My research interests include

  • Information Theory

  • Machine Learning

  • Radar Signal Processing

  • Statistical Signal Processing
